Apps for Board Games

Hex Isle Map

The Settlers of Catan is an exciting multiplayer board game played on a randomly generated map. Hex Isle Map will help you generate a new random map for faster game setup. It supports both pure random and even board generation.

Score Tickets

Counting final score in Ticket to Ride may be time consuming and error prone. Score Tickets will help you to count final score for all players fast and error free.
